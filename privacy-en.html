1. Introduction

Avenia is a mobile application that provides AI-powered chat and productivity services. The privacy and security of our users' personal data is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use our application, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using the application, you agree to the terms specified in this policy.

2. Data Collected

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, Google account ID during registration/login
  • Device and Technical Information: Device model, operating system, app version, unique device identifier
  • Usage Data: Screens viewed, buttons clicked, language preferences, error logs
  • Content and Files: Chat messages, uploaded image/document/video files, data sent to AI
  • Subscription and Payment Data: Purchased packages, billing status (via RevenueCat/RN Purchases)
  • Communication and Support: Error reports sent, support messages, user feedback
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: May be used to improve app performance and experience; can be managed from browser/device settings

3. Data Usage Purposes

  • Account creation, authentication and session management
  • Service delivery, development and personalization
  • Interaction with AI model (OpenAI API) and response generation
  • File upload, storage and sharing (Firebase Storage)
  • Subscription transactions and payment tracking
  • Security, error detection, prevention of misuse
  • Fulfillment of legal obligations
  • Providing support upon user request

4. Data Sharing

Personal data is not shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication, database, file storage), OpenAI (text/visual content sent by the system), RevenueCat / Google Play / Apple App Store (subscription management), analytics and error monitoring services (if any)
  • Legal Requirements: In mandatory situations such as court orders, requests from law enforcement
  • Explicit Consent: When the user gives explicit consent for a specific sharing

Parties with whom data is shared are obligated to protect data privacy.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Data is stored for the duration of service provision or as required by legal obligations. When user account is deleted or legal period expires, personal data is deleted, anonymized or storage period ends.

Uploaded files and chat content can be manually removed by the user; deletion process can also be initiated by sending a request to support@aveniaichat.com.

6. Security

Data is protected with technical/organizational measures such as encryption and access control. However, no system can guarantee 100% security; users are also responsible for protecting their accounts (strong password, device security, etc.).

7. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Correct incorrect or incomplete data
  • Delete or restrict processing of their data within legal conditions
  • Withdraw consent for operations they have consented to
  • Request data portability (to the extent applicable)

You can contact us at support@aveniaichat.com for these requests.

8. Children's Privacy

Avenia does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If data is found to be collected from a child under 13, the account will be closed and data will be deleted.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in different countries through our servers or service providers. In this case, appropriate measures are taken in accordance with relevant data protection laws (e.g. contractual safeguards, standard contractual clauses).

10. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Important changes will be notified within the application or sent to your registered email address. It is the users' responsibility to follow the changes.
